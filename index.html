About Me

Hi, I am kcSeb, and I've mostly worked within the IT sector whether its programming, web-design, systems administration, or content creation, I've done a little bit of it all.

I started out with computers at a really young age, just getting my hands on anything I could to tinker with. Fast forward into my teenage years and I started Have Freedom Hosting. A while after, I joined up with the folks at LBRY and helped them out for a while. I've never been able to sit down and decide what I'd do for a career, and such, I've labeled myself as a free-lance dilettante.
